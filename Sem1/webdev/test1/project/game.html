Short Introduction

... The stone was cold to the touch, but even leaning against it with his naked body, Fanvor did not feel any discomfort. The body did not obey, and with difficulty he brought his numb palms to his mouth in an awkward attempt to loosen the grip of the icy "jaws" with his breath. His lungs were burning, and his whole body seemed to be pierced with needles of frost. One exhalation, another, third ... - the limbs were still trembling involuntarily, but the pain pouring over them in a new wave indicated a returning sensitivity. The man turned his head sharply: “What was that? The sound of my pursuer footsteps? Or a frantic pounding of own heart drumming in your ears? " He involuntarily began to crawl to the side, in the direction of the sound source.

In his head, chaotically flying over one another, the scenarios were spinning. “Maybe I start running? Sprint towards the exit in one movement or try to get to the water? But the body was still poorly obedient and all escape routes were perfectly shot. Can I try to fight? Catch the enemy off guard by jumping out of cover and turn the tide of events with one precise blow? But he was not a skilled fighter and, in addition, lost his weapon in the last failed attack. Try to negotiate? Exit showing empty hands and appeal to the mind of the captive? But the latest attempt to stop the bloodshed only worsened his already dire situation. Surrender to the mercy of the winner? Perhaps a new figure who could speak a human language will be merciful and keep his promise? But it seems that the attackers only need his life

A new sound brought him out of his thoughts, he did not immediately make out it - a quiet splash of water rolling on a stone. The man spent another minute in silent reverie - in such silence he would definitely hear the movement of the enemy - "Perhaps the pursuer rushed for help?" But he remained alone and unarmed, and the sorceress's fighting spirit did not seem to fade away, even after the loss of most of his sisters at the hands of recent captives. Immobile, the guy spent a few more moments in silent concentration, but nothing happened. The ringing silence began to physically crush, and, in the end, Fanvor decided to carefully look out from behind the monolith, behind which he found salvation. Smooth movement: right - clear. With grim anticipation, he repeated the movement, looking over his left shoulder, but there was no one there either. "Am I really alone?" Refusing to believe in such a development of events, the fugitive, breathing deeply, began to slowly rise, carefully studying his surroundings.

Game Characters

Character 1 - Ajay

In appearance, Ajay is a tall youth. Thanks to magic, he does not match his age. It seems that it is important for him: to remain as he was once. His face matches the features of the representatives of the northern kingdom. He has a straight nose, thin lips, the border of which is indistinct.

The oval face is framed by long brown hair. Sometimes you can notice a few gray hairs among this head of hair. His eyes are expressive, the color of sky blue. Ajay's gaze is often attentive and curious.

Character 2 - Sei

Tall, slender, but wiry, one might say, lean, broad-shouldered elf, clearly accustomed to long marches, constant training and battles. Weathered skin and overly dark tan easily betray Sei as a perimeter defender - a ranger. The elf is far from the ideal of appearance that exists among the nobility.

Too angular, unkempt, his face darkened from the wind and sun; the hair, although long, is often gathered in a messy bun, moreover, it is partially faded. In addition to this, as if on purpose, the elf refuses to magically rid himself of the numerous scars and other marks received in battle.

Character 3 - Kettare

A strong, athletic elf with white hair tied back in a braid. The voice is low enough, facial expressions are weak - it is difficult to surprise, make laugh or anger her enough to show a vivid reaction on her face.

Before the battle, he puts on his own forged armor. The choice of weapon is suitable based on the situation - a long two-handed sword, a pike and a double-bladed glaive are usually within her reach, fixed above Kra'el's pack bags.

Character 4 - Garagak

Garagak looks quite typical of an orc: green skin, tall, well-muscled, rough features and brown eyes.The face, despite the rough features, usually in a young orc expresses youthful enthusiasm.

The hair on the head is shaved with a mohawk, the powerful chin is covered with spiky stubble.He also has several tattoos on his body: a bright red tribal symbol flaunts on his right shoulder.

Character 5 - Claudius

A thin man with dark brown eyes. Pale, with small wrinkles on his forehead, Claudius had already managed to change his fifth decade for a long time, although he looked only forty and was relatively cheerful and fresh. The nose was slightly crooked, which only emphasized its Roman profile.

The eyes were very lively, and the unkempt long beard and long white hair lent a certain charm. The expression on the priest's face was intelligently attentive and full of firm humility.

Character 6 - Melissa

Brown, dreaded hair braided to reveal a skinny, tense face. Preaty brown eyes, set sunken within their sockets, watch wearily over the haven they've come to appreciate for so long.Smooth skin beautifully compliments her eyes and mouth and and leaves an intriguing memory of her reckless luck.

The is the face of Melissa Adwell, a true vanquisher among werewolves. She stands alluringly among others, despite her hefty frame.There's something seductive about her, perhaps it's her odd friends or perhaps it's simply her good looks. But nonetheless, people tend to welcome her with open arms, while trying to subtly look more like her.

Game Map

The City of Feryndon

Far to the north in the Kingdom of Rasfadal lies the city of Feryndon, a religious town, ruled by the bishop of the church. The people of Feryndon are devout followers of Herartus, the God of Knowledge.

Tur'Anol Ruins

"Ah! Finally something that prickles my high intellect!" Exclaims Ygzond."The ruins of the past few days were starting to bore me to death!"The party ventures forward towards the abandoned library, but stop in their tracks when a muffled sound can be heard from the floor upstairs...

Wooded Swamp

"As you walk through this swamp area of the wetland, your feet sink deep into the accumulated layers of mud. The mud is held in place by all the old dead leaves and stems of the plants which have died back the winter before. It is dark and blackish and as you disturb it, the scent of rotten eggs wafts up!"

Marketplace

The marketplace is abuzz with activity as vendors and shoppers alike get ready for the Great Bonfire at sundown. Firewood has been gathered, piled, and readied for the great fire to begin.

The mountain lake

Water from the ever-white glaciers above flows down the rocky slopes and gathers in crystal clear pools at the foot of the mountain. A perfect place for a refreshing bath, to make camp after a long day on the road, or to hide the sunken entrance to a vast dwarven dungeon?

Country Road

"Sometimes it's not the destination, but the journey that matters the most."

The Dragonhead Dam

Built to stem the flow of a mighty river, folk claim that real dragon bones were ground up and mixed into the foundation of this mighty dam. Now, a stone dragonhead overlooks the lower basin and reminds passerby that this structure was built for the ages.
