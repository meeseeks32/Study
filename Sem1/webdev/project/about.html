Origin

Our club has grown from a group of like-minded people who have united their love of books and adventure. We organize regular meetings to chat and even argue about the books we have read, play various games and drink tea with goodies in good company. Our mission is to popularize books and role-playing games. We do this by creating a pleasant atmosphere and a sense of community. We are always glad to new members, if you think this place is for you - come, join us!

About author

Since school years TMV was very fond of fairy tales, myths, legends and adventure books. In high school, through the work of Robert Salvatore, he became acquainted with the universe of forgotten realms and through classmates joined the community of role-playing games. Together with friends, they formed a small group in which they discussed books and played board games. TMV plays all kinds of tabletop and role-playing games and actively participates in book club discussions. For him, the club is an opportunity to have fun with friends and help others, fans of fantasy and adventure, to find like-minded people around them.
